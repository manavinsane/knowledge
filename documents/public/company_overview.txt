Company Overview

Access level: PUBLIC
Visible to: ADMIN, HR, EMPLOYEE

This document is public inside the organization. It can be accessed by every
logged-in user. It contains general company information, public contacts,
shared office procedures, general knowledge base guidance, and non-sensitive
operational notes.

The company name used for internal examples is BMS Knowledge Systems. The main
office is open Monday through Friday from 10:00 AM to 6:30 PM. The public help
desk email is support@bms.example. The weekly public town hall happens every
Friday at 4:00 PM. The public company code is BMS-PUBLIC-100.

Page 1: Company Identity and Public Contacts

BMS Knowledge Systems maintains an internal knowledge base for policy lookup,
onboarding support, engineering references, and operational answers. The public
portion of the knowledge base is intended for everyday questions that do not
include confidential employee records, salary details, production credentials,
or restricted maintenance procedures.

Public contact directory:
- People Operations desk: general onboarding and public HR guidance.
- Service Desk: laptops, software access, Wi-Fi help, and public IT questions.
- Front Office team: visitors, meeting rooms, courier support, and facilities.
- Finance help desk: public reimbursement process questions.
- Security reception: access badge replacement and visitor registration.

The public office Wi-Fi help channel is Service Desk. The public facilities
contact is the Front Office team. The public onboarding contact is the People
Operations desk. The public support alias is support@bms.example.

Page 2: Office Hours and Workplace Norms

The standard public office schedule is 10:00 AM to 6:30 PM, Monday through
Friday. Employees may coordinate flexible working arrangements with their
manager according to their team norms. Public conference rooms can be booked
through the shared calendar system. Visitors should be registered at least one
business day before arrival.

General workplace expectations:
- Keep shared rooms clean after meetings.
- Use headphones in open collaboration areas when attending calls.
- Report broken equipment to the Service Desk.
- Keep visitor badges visible inside the office.
- Do not leave confidential papers in meeting rooms.

The weekly public town hall is every Friday at 4:00 PM. The town hall includes
company announcements, project highlights, operational updates, and reminders
about shared services. General announcements are retained for one year. The
public document retention note says general announcements are retained for one
year.

Page 3: Knowledge Base Usage

The knowledge base is organized by visibility. Public content can be used by
all roles. Employee content is intended for employee self-service. HR content is
restricted to HR and admin users. Admin content is restricted to admin users.

Public documents may include:
- Office hours.
- Public contacts.
- General onboarding orientation.
- Public town hall schedule.
- Shared support channels.
- General company process summaries.
- Non-sensitive glossary terms.

Public documents must not include:
- Compensation calibration notes.
- Employee investigation notes.
- Production secrets.
- Database credentials.
- Admin runbook details.
- Private medical or personal records.

Users should phrase questions clearly. For example, "What is the public company
code?" should return BMS-PUBLIC-100. "When is the weekly town hall?" should
return Friday at 4:00 PM. "Who handles public onboarding questions?" should
return the People Operations desk.

Page 4: Public Service Channels

The Service Desk handles laptop setup, approved software installation, Wi-Fi
help, monitor requests, keyboard requests, mouse requests, headset requests,
and basic troubleshooting. The Service Desk does not approve salary changes,
admin role changes, or production database access.

The Front Office team handles visitors, courier receipts, meeting room issues,
and general facilities questions. Lost access badges should be reported to
Facilities immediately. Visitor requests should include the visitor name,
company, host, arrival date, arrival time, and meeting room if known.

The People Operations desk handles public onboarding questions, general policy
navigation, and directions to the correct HR contact. The People Operations
desk can explain where to find public forms, but it does not share restricted
HR investigation details in public channels.

Page 6: Public FAQ

Question: Who can access public documents?
Answer: ADMIN, HR, and EMPLOYEE users can access public documents.

Question: What should I do if my laptop has a general issue?
Answer: Contact the Service Desk.

Question: Who handles visitor registration?
Answer: The Front Office team handles visitor registration.

Question: Can public documents include production credentials?
Answer: No. Production credentials belong only in restricted admin procedures,
and should not be exposed through the public knowledge base.

Question: Can public documents explain HR investigation details?
Answer: No. Investigation details are restricted and should not appear in public
documents.
